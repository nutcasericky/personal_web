I learned about the sport of powerlifting during the Covid-19 lockdown period, and I immediately fell in love with it. I enjoy the adrenaline rushing through my body while I am pushing my limits. I have been posting my training logs on an Instagram account @i_workout_because_we_didnt. I was working part-time in a powerlifting gym where I got to know many people of high capability in the sport, and one of them is my coach @thegymratmat. I am competing in the Singapore Powerlifting Championships as my first meet.
