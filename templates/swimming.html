I started training competitive swimming since I was in primary school. Those days when I used to go to the swimming pool every day for punishing training taught me resilience and unwillingness to give up. During the Covid-19 lockdown, training became inaccessible. However, immediately after the lockdown was lifted, I joined the Pacific Swim Club, trained hard, and participated in many competitions during my Junior College years. Currently, I am coaching under the mentorship of Aquarian Aquatic School. My aspiration is to make knowing how to swim and how to swim well more accessible to young people. I am also a certified lifeguard, which will help me ensure the safety of my trainees and other people in the swimming pool. The following are some of the competitions I took part in, many of which can be found on SwimCloud.

2023 Singapore Swim Series 1 - 33rd in 100m freestyle

2023 Singapore Swim Series 2 - 23rd in 200m freestyle and 24th in 100m butterfly

2023 National School Games A Division - 9th in 200m freestyle and 100m butterfly

2022 National School Games A Division - 4th in 400m freestyle and 7th in 100m butterfly

2022 Pesta Sukan Swimming - 7th in 200m freestyle and 16th in 50m butterfly
